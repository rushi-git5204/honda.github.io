Distinct Styling Cues

Africa twin bears a Twin Lamp LED Headlight, which ensures brighter and richer vision, when the rider is on a dark road. It also complies with the global norm of AHO (Always Headlight On). The LED also helps reduce the power consumption by 90% as compared to conventional Headlamps.

Dynamically responsive

The introduction of TBW greatly expands the choices available for the rider to manage engine output, feel and rear wheel traction. The new HSTC system features 7 Levels – from Level 1 for aggressive riding off-road to Level 7 for maximum sense of security even on slippery, wet tarmac.

Engineered functionality

In DCT, 3 modes of operation are available. Automatic D mode is ideal for city and highway riding and achieves optimum fuel efficiency. Automatic S mode offers 3 levels of sportier riding and shifts down quickly when decelerating for extra engine braking. The MT mode delivers full manual control, allowing the rider to shift with the left handlebar trigger control buttons.

Engineered to Perfection

Equipped with Honda’s unique Dual Clutch Technology, the Africa Twin has a Parallel Twin – 999.11cc Engine with Max Power of 87.7 Bhp@7500 rpm and 93.1 nm Torque@6000 rpm.

  

  

    

    

Ergonomic seating Adjustment

A wide seat at the rear tapers to narrow at the front, which provides extra comfort when sitting plus an easy transition to standing when off-road. The seat can be raised or lowered through a range of 20 mm (840 mm-820 mm).

Exceptional Versatility

The riding modes are comprised of different levels of Power (P) and Engine Braking (EB). There are 3 levels of Power and Engine Braking available as well. TOUR, URBAN GRAVEL Egine Braking and HSTC levels.

          

Innovative Functionality

The African Twin comes equipped with a G Switch, which responds to the unique needs of off-road riding, by providing half-clutch control that responds directly to throttle operation. This feature really comes into its own when negotiating big rifts in the road surface. It also has a unique incline detection feature.

LED Winkers and Taillights

      

The African Twin has Dual-Headlights and Winkers, both LED. The Indicators are now enabled with an Auto Cancel Feature, wherein the system compares the front and rear wheel speed difference and cancels the indicators, relative to the situation.

Rally Style Rider Console

            

            

The Rally Style Rider Console of the African Twin has all the information arranged in definite clusters, making it easy for the eye to pin-point quickly at speed, or o¬ff-road. The negative LCD Display includes - Riding Modes, Speedometer, Tachometer, Fuel-Indicator, Gear Position, HSTC, Odometer, Trip Clock and ABS Indicator.

Superior Suspension Dynamics

Where the road ends, adventure begins. And that’s when you really appreciate the 45 mm Showa Forks. With the longest-in-its-class - 230mm stroke, these inverted cartridge type telescopic forks are fully adjustable with a good range of rebound and compression settings. The rear-shock, coupled with our proven o¬-road Pro-Link system and lightweight swingarm, has a class leading 220mm travel.
